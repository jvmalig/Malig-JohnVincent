Characters

GON FREECSS

Gon is an athletic, rustic, and friendly boy with a thirst for adventure. He is very kind and forgiving, as shown when he chooses to heal a psychopath who had just attempted to kill him.

HISOKA

Hisoka is also shown to be a highly manipulative, cunning, crafty, and deceitful magician. Throughout the series, he wears "women's" shoes and uses an alluring and seductive speech style typically reserved for females in Japanese.

KILLUA ZOLDYCK

Killua is introduced as cheeky, cheerful, and full of mischievous ideas. However, he can be quite rude to others, mostly strangers and older people.

KURAPIKA

Kurapika is a cool and intelligent individual, possessing vast knowledge. He is reticent; not one to open up to people easily and quite judgmental. His distance from others initially keeps him level-headed and moralistic, but his heart has been poisoned with sadness and hatred.

CHROLLO LUCILFER

Chrollo is calm, intelligent, and charismatic, as he seems to be a born leader and able to hold the Troupe together despite the volatile personalities of its members.

LEORIO

Leorio is a tall, manly looking, fairly muscular young man with small brown eyes, black hair, and a spiked crew cut. Despite his mature appearance, he is a teenager. He typically wears a dark blue business suit, black shoes, and a pair of teashade sunglasses.

GING FREECSS

Ging Freecss is a legendary hunter, as well as the former Boar of the zodiac, and Greed Island creator. He is also Kite's mentor and Gon's father. Admired by Satotz for his pioneering archaeological work, Ging been absent for most of the series but recently appeared to take part in the HA election.

PALM SIBERIA

Palm Sirberia is a Hunter and Knov's apprentice. After being captured by the Royal Guards, she is reborn into a Chimera Ant soldier.

ILLUMI ZOLDYCK

Illmui Zoldyck He is generally a stony, ruthless person with an extremely cold way of thinking, reflecting only his own interests. Still, he holds a twisted and overprotective form of love for his younger brother, Killua; this manifests to the point that he used his Nen ability to control Killua and ensure he survives and obeys.

PHINKS

Phinks He is fairly tall and muscular. His most distinguishing features are his piercing eyes and lack of eyebrows.

SHALNARK

Shallnark He is a member of the Phantom Troupe, a powerful group of thieves with unique abilities. Shalnark's power involves controlling and manipulating his enemies using tiny needles he embeds in their bodies.

KALLUTO ZOLDYCK

Kalluto has a silent and obedient personality, often seen doing what he is told. He is established to have a very good relationship with his mother. While he is very quiet, he has shown to be more talkative after joining the Spiders. He is also shown to be quite calm with good control of his feelings.
